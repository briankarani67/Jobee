Our Academic Programs

Bachelor of Education (Science)

A professional program preparing teachers in subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, with emphasis on pedagogy, practicum, and modern teaching approaches.

Bachelor of Education (Arts)

Designed for future educators specializing in subjects like English, Kiswahili, History, CRE, Geography, and Literature. Includes teaching practice and curriculum studies.

B.Ed in Early Childhood & Primary Education

Focuses on child development, early learning theories, instructional strategies, and practicum experiences tailored for early childhood and primary school educators.

Master of Education (M.Ed)

A postgraduate program offering advanced training in educational leadership, curriculum development, research methods, and specialized areas of teaching.

PhD in Education

A research-based doctoral program aimed at producing scholars and experts in education policy, pedagogy, and advanced educational research.
